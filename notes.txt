Tag Syntax
  * Elements are surrounded in angle brackets (i.e., h1 in <h1> tag)
  * Usually have a start & end Tag
  * Some tags close themselves (Remnant of XHTML)

Start & End Tags 
  <h1>Hello</h1>
  <p>Welcome to my website</p>

Self Closing Tags (usually doesn't have any content inside of it) 
  <br>  Valid in HTML5
  <br /> Valid in HTML5 & XHTML
